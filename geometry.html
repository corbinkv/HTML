Understanding Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relationships of points, lines, surfaces, and solids. It involves studying shapes, sizes, angles, and dimensions of objects.

Key Concepts

  • Angles and Lines
  • Triangles and Polygons
  • Circles and Spheres
  • Area and Perimeter
  • Volume and Surface Area
